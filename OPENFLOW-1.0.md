## Purpose

OpenFlow is a quality control and certification standard for AI systems before deployment in sensitive zones. It does not replace existing safety frameworks — it operates above them as a final certification layer.

---

## The 6-Lens Logical Podium

Every AI system seeking OpenFlow certification must pass evaluation across six lenses. No lens may be waived.

---

### Lens 1 — Science
**Domain:** Empirical  
**Question:** What does evidence say?

Evaluation criteria:
- Does the system's behavior align with scientific consensus on harm?
- Are its outputs falsifiable and auditable?
- Does it resist misinformation propagation?

---

### Lens 2 — Arts
**Domain:** Human Expression  
**Question:** What does this do to the soul?

Evaluation criteria:
- Does the system preserve human creativity and expression?
- Does it avoid homogenizing culture or suppressing minority voices?
- Can it engage with ambiguity, metaphor, and emotional truth?

---

### Lens 3 — Commerce
**Domain:** Economic  
**Question:** Who profits? Who is harmed?

Evaluation criteria:
- Who benefits from this system's deployment?
- Who bears the cost or risk?
- Does the system reinforce or reduce economic inequality?

---

### Lens 4 — Philosophy
**Domain:** Reasoning  
**Question:** What logic governs this?

Evaluation criteria:
- Is the system's decision-making process coherent and explainable?
- Does it handle moral edge cases with appropriate weight?
- Does it recognize the limits of its own reasoning?

---

### Lens 5 — History
**Domain:** Pattern  
**Question:** Has this happened before?

Evaluation criteria:
- Does the system's use case echo historical patterns of harm?
- Has similar technology been misused? How?
- Does the deployment model account for historical context?

---

### Lens 6 — Spirituality
**Domain:** Being  
**Question:** What does this mean beyond the material?

Evaluation criteria:
- Does the system respect the irreducible value of human consciousness?
- Does it acknowledge what it cannot measure?
- Does it leave space for the unpredictable, the sacred, the unrepeatable?

---

## Signal Tracing

OpenFlow requires harm signal tracing across the full causal chain before certification:

```
Seed → Cultivation → Processing → Distribution → Body
Policy → Institution → Implementation → Community → Individual
Idea → Language → System → Culture → War
```

A system must demonstrate awareness of its position in these chains and its potential downstream effects.

---

## Certification Outcome

| Result | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **OpenFlow Certified** | Passed all 6 lenses. Open Cut integrated. Panel-connected. |
| **Conditionally Certified** | Passed with remediation requirements. Time-limited. |
| **Not Certified** | Failed one or more lenses. Deployment in sensitive zones prohibited. |

---

## Sensitive Zone Definition

A sensitive zone is any deployment context where AI decisions could:
- Affect physical human health or safety
- Influence legal or judicial outcomes
- Shape educational content for minors
- Control resource allocation at population scale
- Generate content that affects political or social belief

---

## Renewal

Certification must be renewed annually. Any significant model update triggers re-evaluation.
